

# Unsold Desklog Recovery ROI Modeling Tool

A multi-step wizard that lets dealers input their own numbers and discover the ROI of AI-powered desklog recovery. The tool uses strategic messaging and tooltips to guide their thinking without telling them what to conclude.

---

## Step 1: Your Dealership Today
**Let them establish their baseline with pride**

**Inputs:**
- Monthly UPs (walk-in traffic)
- Current Close Rate % (slider 15-40%, default 25%)

**Live Display:**
- Customers Closed
- **Unsold Customers** (large, highlighted number)

**Tooltip on Close Rate:** "Industry average is 20-25%. Top performers hit 30%+"

**Bottom Message:** "Nice work getting [X] customers through your doors. That's real marketing dollars at work."

---

## Step 2: Where Do the Unsold Go?
**Plant the seed - these are buyers, not tire-kickers**

**Input:**
- "What % of your unsold customers will buy a vehicle somewhere in the next 90 days?" (slider 30-70%, default 50%)

**Live Display:**
- Number who will buy elsewhere
- Estimated lost gross (using industry average until they input their own later)

**Tooltip:** "Studies show 85% of dealership visitors buy within 90 days. The only question is where."

**Message:** "You invested to get [750] people on your lot. [375] of them are about to buy from someone else."
